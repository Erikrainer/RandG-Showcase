About Me

Hello! I'm Erik Gontijo, a 25-year-old Brazilian with a knack for languages and a dedication to excellence in my professional endeavors. Fluent in Portuguese, Spanish, and English, I bring a multicultural perspective to my work. With a keen eye for detail and a commitment to fostering talent, I oversee training programs and mentor new team members, ensuring they uphold our tradition of excellence. Additionally, I'm currently enrolled in a bootcamp, further enhancing my skills and knowledge to stay at the forefront of my field. Through this platform, I aim to showcase the expertise and dedication I bring to each carving session and training opportunity, highlighting the professionalism and precision that define my work.
